What Is the V?

At Virginia Tech, we add the V to the T-shaped person through active engagement with the Virginia Tech community and the surrounding region. This will involve purposeful interaction with one’s academic curricula through things like undergraduate research, internships, study abroad, First Year Experience courses, living and learning communities, and more.

There are dozens of options to explore, even just within one college or department, but don’t let that deter you from starting your search! Getting active at the university will enhance your experiences at Virginia Tech, allow you to get ahead of the curve, and help set you up for success during and after college.
