In Two Parts

The \ and the /

At Virginia Tech, we can add the "V" to the T-shaped person through active ivolvement with various activities at Virginia Tech and in the nearby area. This may involve engageing with the curriculum itself through things like undergraduate research, internships, study abroad, First Year Experience courses, living and learning communities, and more. Getting active within the university will allow you to get ahead and help set you up for success both during and after college.
